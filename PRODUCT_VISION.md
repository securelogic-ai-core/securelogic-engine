# PRODUCT_VISION.md

## One-sentence definition
SecureLogic AI is a cyber risk intelligence platform that turns external cyber developments, vendor exposure, AI governance issues, compliance obligations, and internal findings into prioritized action, evidence, and measurable posture for organizations operating under audit.

## Product truth
SecureLogic AI is not a threat feed, not a generic GRC suite, and not a cybersecurity newsletter brand with software attached. It is an operating layer for continuous cyber risk.

## The core problem
Security, GRC, TPRM, compliance, and leadership teams are forced to operate across disconnected systems:
- threat signals live in feeds and inboxes
- vendor risk lives in spreadsheets and questionnaires
- AI governance lives in ad hoc review notes
- compliance obligations live in static trackers
- findings and evidence live in scattered docs and tickets

The result is fragmented judgment, slow action, weak traceability, stale posture, and poor audit readiness.

## What SecureLogic AI does
SecureLogic AI:
1. ingests external cyber, regulatory, vendor, and AI-related signals
2. normalizes and prioritizes those signals
3. connects them to the organization's real operating context:
   - vendors
   - AI systems
   - controls
   - obligations
   - dependencies
   - open risks
   - findings
4. produces decision-ready outputs:
   - actionable recommendations
   - evidence-backed workflows
   - posture views
   - leadership-ready intelligence
   - weekly Intelligence Briefs

## Core platform domains
The platform has five primary domains:
1. Cyber Intelligence
2. Vendor Risk
3. AI Governance
4. Compliance Management
5. Risk and Findings Operations

These are not standalone modules. They are connected operating domains inside one system.

## Who the platform is for
Primary users:
- CISOs and security leaders
- GRC teams
- TPRM leaders and analysts
- compliance and audit-readiness teams
- regulated operators
- security-minded executives who need clear decision support

## The product hierarchy
This hierarchy is fixed:
1. The Platform is the main product.
2. The Intelligence Brief is the entry wedge.
3. Brief Pro is the premium intelligence offer for individuals.
4. Team Professional is the premium intelligence offer for small teams.
5. Platform Professional is the core operating-layer offer.
6. Enterprise is the customized offer for organizations with more complex requirements.

Platform Annual is not a separate product tier. It is the annual billing option for Platform Professional.

## Commercial model
SecureLogic AI currently offers five commercial packages:
- Intelligence Brief — Free
- Brief Pro
- Team Professional
- Platform Professional
- Enterprise

Billing structure:
- Platform Annual is the annual billing option for Platform Professional, not a standalone tier.

## The Intelligence Brief
The Intelligence Brief is not a generic news digest.
It is a weekly intelligence product that synthesizes what changed, why it matters, what exposure it creates, and what should happen next.

The Brief should:
- compress reading time
- provide judgment, not just information
- identify urgency
- route action by audience
- connect signals to platform context where available

The Brief gets users into the SecureLogic AI ecosystem.
The Platform is where that intelligence becomes workflow, evidence, and posture.

## The Platform
The SecureLogic AI Platform is where organizations:
- track vendors, AI systems, obligations, controls, dependencies, risks, and findings
- attach evidence and actions
- connect external signals to internal exposure
- run reassessment workflows
- produce dashboards and posture views
- operate in a way that is explainable to clients, auditors, and leadership

## What makes the platform valuable
The value is not raw information.
The value is decision compression and operational traceability.

SecureLogic AI should save customers from:
- reading dozens of disconnected updates
- missing relevant exposure
- manually translating signals into internal action
- maintaining fragmented evidence
- being unprepared for audit or client review

## What the final product experience must feel like
The final product should feel:
- premium
- enterprise-grade
- clear
- operational
- defensible under audit
- more like a mission system than a dashboard toy

A customer should feel:
- I know what matters now.
- I know what to do next.
- I can prove why we acted.
- I can explain our posture to leadership and auditors.
- This replaces fragmented workflows with one operating layer.

## What SecureLogic AI is not
SecureLogic AI is not:
- a generic alert feed
- a lightweight blog or content brand
- a generic GRC admin panel
- a vendor questionnaire repository only
- a compliance checklist app
- an AI governance spreadsheet replacement only
- a feature pile without operational coherence

## Final product mandate
Every feature, workflow, read surface, and AI output must reinforce the core truth:
SecureLogic AI is the operating layer for continuous cyber risk.
