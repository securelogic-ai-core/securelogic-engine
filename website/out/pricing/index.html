Common questions

When is the Intelligence Brief delivered?

The Intelligence Brief is generated and delivered weekly. Subscribers receive it by email and can also access it via the platform.

What sources does the Intelligence Brief cover?

The brief draws from security advisories, regulatory publications, vendor risk feeds (SecurityWeek, Dark Reading), AI governance sources, and additional threat intelligence channels.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Cancel at any time. Your access continues through the end of the billing period.

When will the full platform (Vendor Risk, AI Governance, Compliance) be available?

These modules are in active development. Enterprise customers get early access. Contact us for a roadmap conversation.

Is there an API for risk intelligence data?

Yes, as part of the Enterprise tier. Contact us to discuss your integration requirements.
